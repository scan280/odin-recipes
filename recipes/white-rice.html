Rice recipe

Description

Savor a fragrant jasmine rice dish, perfectly steamed to fluffy perfection. Enhanced with a touch of coconut milk and a hint of salt, this subtly sweet and aromatic rice complements a variety of cuisines. Ideal as a side dish or a base for stir-fries and curries, it brings a delightful texture and flavor to your meal.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Coconut milk
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Optional:Fresh herbs/lime zest for garnish

Steps

  1. Rinse the jasmine rice under cold water
  2. Combine rice, coconut milk, water, and salt in a pot
  3. Bring to a boil over medium heat
  4. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender
  5. Remove from heat and let it sit covered for a few minutes
  6. Fluff with a fork and garnish with fresh herbs or lime zest if desired
