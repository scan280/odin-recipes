Kales recipe

Description

Enjoy a vibrant bowl of kale, featuring tender, sautéed greens seasoned with garlic, onions, and a hint of chili for a delightful kick. This nutritious dish is not only packed with vitamins but also brings a satisfying earthiness, making it a perfect side for any meal or a nutritious main when paired with grains or proteins.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Chili flakes
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Optional: Lemon juice or sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Olive oil in a pan
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent
  3. Stir in minced garlic and chili flakes, cooking for a minute
  4. Add the kale or skuma wiki and cook until wilted
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  6. Remove from heat and add optional lemon
    7. juice or sesame seeds before serving
