Green Grams Recipe

Description

A delightful green gram curry features tender mung beans simmered with aromatic spices like cumin and turmeric, along with onions, tomatoes, and fresh herbs. This nutritious dish is rich in protein and flavor, making it perfect served with rice or flatbreads. It’s easy to prepare and can be customized with your favorite veggies or spices!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup green grams (mung beans), soaked for 4 hours
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1-2 green chilies, slit (optional)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • 2 tbsp oil

Steps

  1. Soak 1 cup of green grams (mung beans) in water for about 4 hours. Drain and rinse.
  2. a large pot, heat 2 tablespoons of oil over medium heat. Add 1 teaspoon of cumin seeds and let them sizzle for a few seconds.
  3. 1 finely chopped onion and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Stir in 2-3 minced garlic cloves and 1-inch grated ginger. Cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  5. Add 2 chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and start to break down.
  6. Mix in 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder and optional 1-2 slit green chilies. Cook for another minute.
  7. Stir in the soaked mung beans and mix well with the spices.
  8. Add 3 cups of water (adjust based on desired consistency) and salt to taste. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until the beans are tender.
  9. Once the beans are cooked, add 1 teaspoon of garam masala and mix well. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  10. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh cilantro. Serve with rice or flatbreads. Enjoy!
