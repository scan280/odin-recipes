Green Grams Recipe

Description

A delightful green gram curry features tender mung beans simmered with aromatic spices like cumin and turmeric, along with onions, tomatoes, and fresh herbs. This nutritious dish is rich in protein and flavor, making it perfect served with rice or flatbreads. Itâ€™s easy to prepare and can be customized with your favorite veggies or spices!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup green grams (mung beans), soaked for 4 hours
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1-2 green chilies, slit (optional)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • 2 tbsp oil
